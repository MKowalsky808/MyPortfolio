About Me

I hold a Bachelor's in Communications and Associate of Applied Science in Court Reporting. I am certified at 225 wpm on my stenographic machine. I love learning new languages, whether that's steno shorthand, Japanese, and now SQL. I am currently learning Python programming language. I am a lifelong learner and ready for my next big challenge as a data analyst.
